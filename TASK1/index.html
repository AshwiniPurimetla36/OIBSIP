HAVE A DRINK

A soft drink is a drink that usually contains water, a sweetener, and a natural or artificial flavoring.
In 1767, the first drinkable man-made carbonated water was created by Englishman Joseph Priestley.
Three years later, Swedish chemist Torbern Bergman invented
generating apparatus that made carbonated water from chalk using sulfuric acid.
